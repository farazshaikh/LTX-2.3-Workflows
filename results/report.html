Prompting Principles Applied

  • Face-forward keyframes -- all frames have the subject facing the camera with clear facial features to prevent AI face hallucination
  • "Describe what you see + one subtle motion" -- i2v prompts precisely describe the source image content and add only minimal motion cues
  • No object injection -- nothing appears in prompts that isn't already visible in the keyframes (prevents scene drift)
  • Frame-to-frame coherence -- middle derived from first, last derived from middle. Same woman, dress, background throughout
  • Minimal transitions -- only head turn and expression change between frames, no scene/location shifts
